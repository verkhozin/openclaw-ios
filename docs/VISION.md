# Vision

Not another AI chat app. A native iOS command center where your AI agent works FOR you, and you steer with taps and voice.

The phone is not where you work with the agent. It's where the agent works for you, and you approve, redirect, and capture thoughts on the go.

## Core Principles

- Voice-first input, visual-first output
- One-tap actions over typing
- Agent runs in background, you get results
- Native iOS citizen: widgets, Live Activities, Siri, Share Sheet
- Privacy-first: explicit toggles for every data source
